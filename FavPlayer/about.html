About Ryan O'Reilly

Ryan O'Reilly was born on February 7, 1991 in Clinton, Ontario, Canada
He is 6ft 1in and weighs 216lbs

He started his career with the Seaforth Stars of the Ontario Minor Hockey Association
During the 2009-10 season, he became the youngest player to appear in a regular season game for the Colorado Avalanche hockey team
In 2015, the Avalanche traded him to the Buffalo Sabres where he got the most faceoff wins in a single season in 2018
In 2018 he was traded once again into the St Louis Blues as a defensive forward
In 2020 Ryan O'Reilly became captain of the St Louis Blues

Ryan O'Reilly won the Frank J. Selke Trophy for best defensive forward in 2019
